07

Full Integration · How it works

Two Zaps run
in the background.

The integration runs through Zapier, a trusted automation platform used by hundreds of thousands of businesses. You'll have your own Zapier account, with your own credentials, fully under your control.

Zap 01

CosmoLex → Donna

New and updated matters flow into Donna automatically. The matter list stays in sync. No CSV refreshes, no manual upkeep.

Zap 02

Donna → CosmoLex

Released timecards post to CosmoLex with matter, timekeeper, and billing type already mapped. No double entry, no copy-paste.

You own the connection · Your credentials, your account · Donna subscription discount offsets Zapier cost

    Generate CosmoLex API credentials

    Firm admin pulls API credentials from CosmoLex's settings.

    Connect Donna and CosmoLex inside Zapier

    Paste your CosmoLex credentials and authorize Donna. Both Zaps wired in one place.

    One timecard end-to-end. Confirm it lands on the right matter, timekeeper, and billing type.

    Map timekeepers

    Match each Donna user to their CosmoLex timekeeper. About five seconds per person.

  6. 06

    Walk through the daily flow

    Capture → review → release. The first real entry runs together, on the call.
