How It Works

Three steps. Zero effort.

01

Open Donna and work

Donna runs in the background while you draft motions, research case law, and handle client calls. It observes your active window and captures periodic screenshots for AI context.

02

AI generates your timesheet

When you stop tracking, Donna's AI produces categorized billing entries with professional narratives from your activity data. All raw data is immediately destroyed.

03

Review, edit, and export

Review AI-generated entries, adjust narratives if needed, assign to client matters, and export a clean timesheet to CSV. Donna handles the paperwork so you can handle the cases.
